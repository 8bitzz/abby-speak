Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Abby Speak (the “App”) handles information when you use it. The App is designed for language learning and does not require you to create an account.

In brief. We do not use advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. Your learning progress and preferences stay on your device unless you choose to use a service outside the App, such as sending a support email or opening a third-party website.

1. Information stored on your device

The App stores the following information locally on your device to provide its features:

  • favorite vocabulary and saved learning preferences;
  • practice activity, including practiced question identifiers, dates, counts, and streaks;
  • daily reminder preferences, such as whether reminders are enabled and the selected time; and
  • downloaded or cached audio used for playback.

This information is not transmitted to our servers by the App. You can remove it by deleting the App and its associated data from your device.

2. Purchases

If you purchase a subscription or other in-app product, Apple processes the payment and manages your subscription through StoreKit. The App uses purchase and entitlement status to unlock features. We do not receive or store your payment-card information.

3. Audio delivery

The App can download or stream learning audio from an external content-delivery provider. As with any internet request, that provider may process technical request information, such as your IP address and device or browser information, to deliver the audio. We do not use those requests to create a profile of you, serve advertising, or track you across apps or websites.

4. Notifications

If you enable daily practice reminders, the App asks for notification permission and schedules notifications locally on your device. The App does not use remote push notifications or collect a device push token.

5. Support email

Choosing “Contact Support” opens your email app with a message addressed to hang@proxyman.com. If you send us an email, we receive the information you choose to include, such as your email address and message. We use it only to respond to your request and retain it only as long as needed for support, record-keeping, or legal obligations.

6. Sharing, selling, and tracking

We do not sell personal information. We do not use the App for targeted advertising, and we do not track you across apps or websites. We do not share your on-device learning data with third parties. Apple, content-delivery providers, and email providers may handle information as described in their own policies when you use their respective services.

7. Your choices

You can disable notifications in the App or iOS Settings. You can delete locally stored learning data by deleting the App and its associated data. To request deletion of a support email or ask a privacy question, contact us at hang@proxyman.com.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the App or applicable requirements change. The “Last updated” date above indicates when it was most recently revised.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact hang@proxyman.com.
